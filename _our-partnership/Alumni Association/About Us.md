<h2>Primary 1 Phase 2A(1) Registration</h2>
<p>To be eligible for the Primary 1 Phase 2A(1) registration, you will need
to be a full member of the alumni association at least one year before
the 30th of June of the year when your child needs to be registered. This
is in accordance to MOE guidelines.</p>
<p>Example:</p>
<p>If your child needs to be registered in 2020 (to begin school in 2021),
you will need to be a full member of the association before 30th June 2019.</p>
<p>For more information, visit: <a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.moe.gov.sg/admissions/primary-one-registration/phases</a>
</p>
