<h5>Processes/Systems in JWPS to Support Students</h5><ul data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p>Withdrawal, in-class and recess support, specialised remediation for students in the areas of learning, behavioural, as well as social emotional needs</p></li><li><p>Implementation of Individual Education Plans for students with high needs</p></li><li><p>Data-driven decision-making processes to inform students’ support and progress</p></li><li><p>Consultation to teachers to facilitate better understanding of the students’ needs and how to support them.</p></li><li><p>Case conferences with teachers and within the Case Management team to discuss support for students</p></li><li><p>Capacity building sessions for teachers and workshops for parents</p></li><li><p>Close home-school collaboration between teachers and parents</p></li><li><p>Collaboration with and/ or referral to MOE Educational Psychologists, external agencies and hospitals, and special differently</p></li><li><p>Continues throughout life</p></li><li><p>Unlikely that a student will “suddenly” develop dyslexia</p></li><li><p>Cannot be cured but symptoms can be
