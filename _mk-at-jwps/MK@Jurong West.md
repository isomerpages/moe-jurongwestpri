<p>
<br>Parents who send/fetch their child outside the above gate opening hours
are advised to call/WhatsApp the respective numbers (96791940 – KCare /
96474301 - MK) first. Do note that the phone is manned only during office
hours only.
<br>
</p>
<p>As the KCare and MK staff will be busy taking care of all the children,
you may have to wait at the foyer for up to <strong>15 minutes</strong> if
you send/fetch your child outside the above gate opening hours.</p>
<p>The security guard’s main role is to maintain safety and security. They
are <strong>not required</strong> to call the MK/KCare staff on parents’
behalf.</p>
<h5>MK/KCare Premise: No entry for parents/caregivers</h5>
<p>For safety and security reasons, all parents/caregivers are NOT allowed
to enter the MK/KCare premise. We seek your co-operation for the safety
of your child and all other children.</p>
<h5>Park at Blk 601A Multi-storey Carpark</h5>
<p>For parents or caregivers who drive, you are strongly encouraged to park
at the visitor lots inside block 601A multi-storey carpark.</p>
<p>For more information about MK and its curriculum, please refer to the
<a href="https://moe.gov.sg/preschool/moe-kindergarten/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">MOE MK website</a>.</p>
