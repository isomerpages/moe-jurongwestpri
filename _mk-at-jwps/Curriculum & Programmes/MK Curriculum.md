<h3><strong>Learning at MK@JW</strong></h3>
<p>Our curriculum is anchored on two flagship programmes:</p>
<p>📖 <strong>HI-Light Programme</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Supporting children’s holistic development through integrated and active
learning.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 90%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/MK/HI_Light_Prog.jpg">
</div>
<p></p>
<p>📖 <strong>Starlight Literacy Programme</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Nurturing bilingualism in English and Mother Tongue Languages (Chinese,
Malay, or Tamil).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Each school term also culminates in a Weeks of Wonder (WOW) project, where
children explore, inquire, and apply their learning through meaningful,
hands-on experiences.</p>
</li>
</ul>
