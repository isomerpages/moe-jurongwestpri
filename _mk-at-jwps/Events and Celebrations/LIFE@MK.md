<h3>🧩 <strong>L</strong>eading and Inspiring <strong>F</strong>amilies of <strong>E</strong>arly Learners @ <strong>M</strong>OE <strong>K</strong>indergarten (LIFE@MK) Programme</h3>
<p>Families play an important role in nurturing children’s holistic development.
We routinely involve families in children’s learning through our series
of workshops and sharing sessions under the LIFE@MK programme.</p>
<p>Our MK educators will share:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>What your child learns at MK.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Teaching and learning approaches that are appropriate for your child’s
development.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Practical tips to help you nurture your child's holistic development at
home.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Information on specific topics, such as supporting your child’s transition
to primary school.</p>
</li>
</ul>
