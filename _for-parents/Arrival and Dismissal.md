<h2>Arrival</h2>
<h4>Pedestrian</h4>
<p>Students can enter the school via Gate 1, 2, 3 and 4 in the morning. Gate
3 will not be opened if the weather is bad.</p>

</div>
<h4>Vehicles</h4>
<p>Parents who send their child to school by car should follow the instructions
of the 2 guards stationed at the main gate and enter the school as illustrated
below:</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/AM%20car%20arrival.jpg">
</div>
<p>Parents should not park along Jurong West Street 61 and let their child
alight by the roadside.</p>
<h2>Dismissal</h2>
<p><strong>No parents are allowed to drive into the school during dismissal time</strong>.</p>
<p>Parents should park at the nearby multi-storey carpark and not park along
Jurong West Street 61 to wait for their child.</p>
<p><strong>All students are dismissed at 1:30pm.</strong>
</p>
<h4>P1 and P2</h4>
<p>P1 students are dismissed at the <strong><u>Polyclinic Gate</u></strong>.</p>
<p>P2 students are dismissed at <strong><u>Gate 2</u></strong>.</p>
<p></p>
<h4>P3, P4, P5 and P6</h4>
<p>P3, P4, P5 and P6 students are allowed to use any gates to go home.</p>
